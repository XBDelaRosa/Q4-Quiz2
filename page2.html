CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE

Crisostomo Ibarra
  • An intelligent young bachelor who studied in Europe for 7 years
  • The Fiance of Maria Clara
  • Son of the late Don Rafel Ibarra

Maria Clara
  • A lovely, sweet and compassionate maiden
  • The Fiance of Crisostomo Ibarra
  • Daugher of Dona Pia Alba and Capitan Tiago

Don Rafel Ibarra
  • A kind and helpful man who loved his country
  • He is the richest man in all of San Diego
  • Is known to be a heretic and filubuster

Capitan Tiago
  • A well-known socialite and rich aristocrat from the town of San Diego
  • Has a close relationship with prominent clergymen like Father Salvi and Father Dámaso
  • He is the father of Maria Clara

Father Damaso
  • He is known to be a snobby, violent, vicious, and judgmental priest
  • He is the former curate of San Diego and a Fransiscan Spanish Friar
  • He is the biological father of Maria Clara

Father Salvi
  • Father Salvi succeeded Padre Damaso as the town curate of San Diego
  • He is a conscientious and cunning man who exploits his position as a priest to influence politics for the advantage of both the church and himself
  • He is a Fransiscan Friar who fell in love with Maria Clara

Sisa
  • Sisa is a destitute, distraught lady who has been through a lot
  • The mother of the two sacristans Crispin and Basilio
  • She has been physically abused by her husband Pedro

Basilio
  • A 10 year old young boy who works for the priest in the church
  • Basilio showed awareness at an early age, understanding his father's mistreatment to his mother
  • As he grew older, Basilio had aspirations of working as a herdsman for Crisostomo Ibarra to help his family escape poverty

Crispin
  • Crispin is one of the sacristans of the church and the younger brother of Basilio
  • Was accused of stealing 2 ounces from Father Salvi
  • He was brutally beaten by the Sacristan Mayor leading to his death

Philosopher Tasyo
  • Started to develop a sign of mental illness after his beloved wife's death
  • He was a wise and knowledgeable person
  • Tasyo dedicated his entire life to learning, even to the point of letting his spending on books cause him to fall into poverty
